Meet Our Partners

Our Previous Projects

AdLens

AdLens is a project from some volunteers who track and monitor political advertising, mainly on facebook.

We plan to turn AdLens in a commercial platform/company in 2023.

Role: Data gathering (regular and irregular), reporting, analysis. (2021 - )

Tools:

  • NodeJS
  • GCS
  • Google BigQuery
  • MongoDB

TextToTree

TextToTree is an online tool that creates unique tree images as a representation of the emotional content of text messages.

Role: algorithm development, text analysis, implementation (2019 - )

Tools:

  • NodeJS
  • MongoDB
  • 3D.js
  • Sentiment analysis API's (amazon, google, microsoft)

Storedesk

Storedesk offers premium SMS services to companies. Storedesk has direct connections to the Belgian telecom providers and processes these messages with a custom build platform. Storedesk processed over 100M premium SMS messages.

  • TV onscreen chat
  • Teletext chat
  • TV onscreen games
  • TV onscreen voting
  • Weather forecast
  • Uptime alerts
  • micropayments / web paywall

Tools:

  • NodeJS
  • GCS
  • Google BigQuery
  • MongoDB

LOSO

LOSO was a longitudinal study of the KULeuven (department of educational studies) in Belgian secondary schools.

Role (1994-1996): Data cleanup, database design
